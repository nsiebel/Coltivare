Coltiview

Our software system Coltiview provides our users with all kinds of feedback for both crops and livestock. When the images are uploaded to the software from the drone/camera, we give feedback on damaged areas or areas that are starting to crow in faster and are closer to being harvested compared to other crops on the farm. From a livestock standpoint, we give feedback if one of your animals is sick or possibly pregnant.


How it Works

Even though our products consist of highly sophisticated engineering and technology, products from Coltivare are very simple to use.

  1. If purchased, install Coltiview software wherever you want
  2. Pair the drone/camera with the system
  3. Set up drone/camera in area you want to observe
  4. Take image and our system will provide you with feedback
